Global Covid-19 Data Visualization from 2020 to 2023

Daily number of Covid-19 Confirmed cases in Malaysia from January 2020 to September 2023

The daily number of Confirmed cases in Malaysia has increased significantly since October 2020 and reached its peak in March 2022. However, the number of daily Confirmed cases has decreased gradually after April 2022.

Top 5 countries with the highest cumulative sum of comfirmed cases by the end of July 2020

US (United States of America) has the highest number of confirmed cases which is over 4 million followed by Brazil, India, Russia and lastly by Peru with more than 300000 cases. 3 out of 5 countries are from Americas region which are US, Brazil and Peru.

The ranking of countries in new Covid-19 cases in each month starting from January 2020 to December 2022

We might be interested to know which country has the highest number of confirmed cases in each month. We observe that China and Russia has the highest number of confirmed cases at the start and end of 2020 respectively.

Furthermore, at the end of 2021, Russia is ranked number 1 ,while China is ranked number 1 at the end of 2022.

Interactive plot showing relationship and trend between Comfirmed and Death cases across different continents.

The scatter plot shows a very strong positive correlation between Comfirmed and Death cases. As the number of Comfirmed cases increases, we are expected to see an increase in the number of Death cases.

Overall, the more population a country has, the more Comfirmed and Death cases it has due to the higher chance of transmission.

The number of comfirmed cases in China, Indonesia, Japan, Malaysia and Singapore

It is obvious that Malaysia has the lowest number of cases and China has the highest number of cases among these five countries in 2020.

Total number of Covid-19 tests by different continents

Europe has the highest number of tests. While Asia and North America has roughly the same amount of tests. The Oceania has the least amount of tests due to least population.

Global Covid-19 Death per 1 Million Population in 2020

Countries located in North America have a higher death rate per 1 million population compare to countries in other regions. America , Mexico and Canada are having a death rate of more than 100 people.
